Zone Alert — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Zone Alert ("the app") alerts drivers when they approach or enter reduced-speed zones such as school and playground zones. This policy describes what data the app handles. The short version: your location never leaves your device, and we do not collect personal information.

Location data

The app uses your device's precise location, while the app is in use, to detect nearby zones, trigger alerts, and show your position on the map. All location processing happens on your device. Your location is never transmitted to us or to any third party by the app, and it is not stored beyond what is needed to show your recent alert history on your device.

Data stored on your device

Your settings, custom zones, and alert history are stored only on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes them.

Zone data downloads

The app periodically downloads municipal speed-zone data snapshots from our public data repository (hosted on GitHub) or directly from city open-data services. These are ordinary HTTPS downloads; the hosting providers may log standard request metadata (such as IP address) per their own policies. No personal identifier from the app is included in these requests.

Advertising

The free version shows banner ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads, as described in Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data practices. Purchasing the one-time premium unlock removes all advertising.

Purchases

Premium purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Accounts and analytics

The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs beyond the advertising described above.

Children

The app is intended for drivers and is not directed at children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
